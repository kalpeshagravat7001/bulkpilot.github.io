BulkPilot – Bulk Mail Sender Pro

The simple and powerful app that helps you send emails to multiple recipients using Gmail SMTP efficiently and securely.

Get it on Google Play Learn More

Powerful Features

Designed for efficiency, control, and better delivery.

🚀

Bulk Sending

Easily add multiple email addresses and send emails in one go. Ideal for job applications, outreach, and communication.

⏱️

Smart Delay System

Control how emails are sent with delay options (10s, 30s, 1m+). Improves delivery and reduces spam risk.

✍️

Custom Content

Create your own subject and message. HTML content is fully supported for professional formatting.

📎

Attach Files

Attach documents like PDFs and send them to multiple recipients quickly and easily.

🔐

Secure SMTP

Uses Gmail SMTP with App Password authentication. Credentials stay on your device and are never shared.

⚡

Background Sending

Emails can be sent in the background while you continue using your device for other tasks.

📊

Real-time Progress

Track how many emails are sent, pending, or failed in real time with our intuitive dashboard.

🔒

Privacy First

We do not store or share your email data. All operations are performed locally on your device.
